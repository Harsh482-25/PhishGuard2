Why Phishing Awareness Matters

Phishing attacks are among the most common and damaging cyber threats facing individuals and organizations today. Attackers continuously evolve their tactics, using social engineering to exploit human psychology.

71% of organizations faced BEC attacks in 2022
90% of data breaches start with phishing
60% increase in targeted phishing attempts

Types of Phishing Attacks

Email Phishing

Mass-sent fraudulent emails pretending to be from legitimate sources.

Spear Phishing

Highly targeted attacks using personalized information about the victim.

Smishing & Vishing

Phishing via SMS (smishing) or voice calls (vishing).

Effective training combines education with practical simulations, helping users develop the skills to identify and avoid real threats. PhishGuard addresses this need by providing engaging, hands-on experience in a risk-free environment.
